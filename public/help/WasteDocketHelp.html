Overview

The WasteDocket system revolutionizes waste management by leveraging mobile technology, allowing businesses to manage waste collection efficiently directly from their smartphones. This application not only promotes a sustainable future by minimizing paper use but also facilitates the digital transformation of traditional waste management practices.

By integrating digital dockets, it helps reduce operational costs, streamline back-office processes, and simplifies annual reporting with its cloud-based solutions, making it accessible without the steep learning curve often associated with similar technologies.

Brief Description

Businesses

  • Business Management: Businesses involved in waste collection can easily manage operations through the WasteDocket system. They can sign up via web or mobile apps, complete account verification through a one-time password (OTP), and add essential business information like VAT, permit numbers, and contact details.
  • Functionality: After logging in, businesses have a dashboard where they can manage drivers, customers, dockets, and destination facilities. They can also view and upload waste permit documents, managing their profile and business information seamlessly.

Drivers

  • Driver Signup and Management: Drivers can sign up by providing their email and other details, accepting terms and conditions, and going through the OTP verification. Businesses invite drivers via email, and drivers can accept or reject these invites.
  • Operational Ease: Once logged in, drivers have access to their dashboard to add and view dockets, manage their profile, and check details about the business they are associated with. This allows drivers to be efficiently integrated into the waste management process.

Dockets

  • Core Function: Dockets are digital forms that record all necessary information about a waste pickup job, including customer details, waste specifics, collection and destination points, and required signatures.
  • Comprehensive Management: Businesses can add new dockets with details about the customer, the type of waste, collection points, and the destination facility. Drivers can also add and manage dockets, ensuring that all job-related details are accurately captured and maintained in real-time. Dockets can be added through a web form or directly via the mobile app.

Additional Features

  • Subscription Plans: The application offers various subscription plans ranging from basic to enterprise levels, catering to different business sizes and needs.
  • Help and Support: A dedicated help section with FAQs provides guidance on common tasks like adding drivers, upgrading plans, or changing customer addresses.

Overall, WasteDocket enhances the efficiency of waste management operations by reducing paperwork, streamlining back-office processes, and facilitating better communication and data management through its cloud-based solutions.

Business

Businesses can register through either the web or mobile applications by providing the full name and email address of the primary contact and agreeing to the terms and conditions. Following registration, the account verification process is initiated via a 6-digit OTP sent to the provided email.

Once verified, businesses are required to input additional information such as the business name, VAT details, and permit information.

The home page then serves as a central dashboard where businesses can manage various operational aspects including driver and customer management, dockets, and destination facilities, as well as view and upload waste permit documents, ensuring efficient and streamlined business operations.

Docket Management

Add Dockets

The "Add Dockets" functionality is a vital component of the WasteDocket system, allowing businesses to create new dockets for waste collection services. This feature enables you to comprehensively capture all necessary details related to a specific waste pickup, ensuring that each collection is documented and managed effectively.

Steps to Add a New Customer Docket:

  • Entering Customer Information:
  • Start by adding the customer's details. This includes the customer’s name, contact information, and address. If the customer is already in the system, you can select their name from a dropdown list; otherwise, you may enter the new customer's details manually.

  • Waste Information:
  • Specify the type of waste to be collected. This section should include the waste classification, any relevant waste codes, and whether the waste is hazardous. This information is crucial for ensuring that the waste is handled and processed correctly.

  • Collection Point Details:
  • Input the exact location from where the waste will be collected. This might be the customer’s primary business location or another specified site. Details should include the full address and any specific instructions or gate codes required to access the site.

  • Destination Facility:
  • Select the facility where the waste will be processed or disposed of. This could be a recycling center, landfill, or other waste treatment facilities. The system allows you to choose from existing facilities or add a new one if necessary.

  • Load of Export (if applicable):
  • If the waste is to be exported, details about the port of export, destination country, and receiving facility need to be included. This is critical for compliance with international waste transfer regulations.

  • Driver and Waste Facility Signature:
  • Finally, capture the signatures of the driver collecting the waste and a representative from the destination facility. This step is necessary to confirm that the information is accurate and that both parties agree to the terms of waste transfer.

  • Completing the Docket Creation:
  • Once all the necessary information is filled in, review the details for accuracy and completeness.

  • Submit the docket for processing. The system will generate a unique docket number for tracking and future reference.

Add Dockets - Add Waste

The "Add Waste" function within the Add Dockets process on WasteDocket is crucial for accurately recording details about the waste being collected. This feature allows users to input specific information about each type of waste involved in a collection, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and facilitating efficient waste management.

Key Functions of the Add Waste Feature:

  • Entering Waste Details:
  • Users can add comprehensive information about the waste, such as type, category, quantity, and whether it is hazardous. This data helps in categorizing and handling waste according to regulatory standards and safety protocols.

  • Documentation and Compliance:
  • Critical for maintaining proper records, the system allows for the inclusion of waste codes, descriptions, and any necessary compliance details, such as handling instructions and disposal methods.

  • Streamlining Waste Management Processes:
  • By providing detailed information, this feature ensures that waste is processed and disposed of in the most appropriate and environmentally friendly manner.

   

Add Dockets - Selection Screen

The "Selection Screen" is a pivotal part of the Add Dockets process on WasteDocket, featuring two key components: Waste Codes and Waste Unit selection.

  • Waste Codes
  • Users are presented with an organized list of waste codes to classify the type of waste being processed. This list ensures that users can accurately categorize waste according to regulatory standards. A search bar at the top allows for quick filtering, making it easy to find and select the correct code.

  • Select Waste Unit
  • Users can specify the unit of measurement for the waste quantity, such as kilograms (KG), liters (L), tonnes, or other units. This flexibility ensures precise reporting and standardization across various types of waste and collection requirements.

Drivers

Driver Signup

The "Driver Signup" feature within WasteDocket enables new drivers to easily join your fleet, streamlining the process of expanding your driving resources. This user-friendly registration process ensures that all necessary information is collected to maintain a secure and compliant operation.

Steps for Driver Signup:

  • Filling Out the Signup Form:
  • Drivers begin the registration process by accessing the signup form, where they are required to enter their full name and a valid email address. This information is essential for creating a unique profile within the WasteDocket system and for future communications.

  • Accepting Terms and Conditions:
  • To proceed with the signup, drivers must read and accept the terms and conditions. This is done by checking a box on the form, signifying their agreement to comply with company policies and legal guidelines.

  • Submission and OTP Verification:
  • After submitting the signup form, drivers are automatically redirected to the OTP (One-Time Password) verification page. Here, they will receive a 6-digit code sent to their registered email address, which must be entered to verify their identity and activate their account. This step is crucial for security purposes and ensures that only authorized personnel access the system.

        

  • Completing the Signup Process:
  • Upon successful OTP verification, the driver's account becomes active, allowing them to log in and access their new dashboard. From here, they can view assignments, manage schedules, and utilize other tools provided by WasteDocket to enhance their workflow.

Upgrading Plans

Upgrading your subscription plan in WasteDocket allows you to access enhanced features and capabilities tailored to support the growth and expansion of your business operations. The process is designed to be straightforward and secure, ensuring that you can quickly benefit from the additional services.

Step-by-Step Guide to Upgrade Your Subscription:

  • Navigating to Account Settings:
  • Log in to your WasteDocket dashboard and navigate to the 'Account' section. This area centralizes all your account-related settings and subscription details.

  • Selecting a New Plan:
  • Within the Account settings, locate and click on the “Upgrade” option. This will display the various subscription plans available, including descriptions and pricing of each. Review these options carefully to select the plan that best meets your evolving business needs.

  • Choosing and Confirming Your New Plan:
  • Once you have decided on a new plan, select it. You may be prompted to confirm your choice or to review the new plan benefits and terms before proceeding.

  • Entering Payment Information:
  • After confirming your plan selection, you will be directed to a secure payment page. Here, you will need to enter your payment details, such as credit card or bank information, required to process the upgrade. Ensure all details are accurate to avoid any delays or issues with your upgrade.

  • Completing the Subscription Upgrade:
  • Review all the information one last time for accuracy and then submit your upgrade request. You will receive a confirmation once the payment is processed, and your account will be immediately updated to reflect your new subscription plan.

  • Confirmation and Access to New Features:
  • Following a successful upgrade, a confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address. Log out and back into your dashboard to ensure all new features and enhancements are activated and accessible under your new plan.

After Upgrading Your Plan:

  • Review New Features: Familiarize yourself with the enhanced features and tools that are now available to you.

  • Adjust Settings: Depending on the plan, adjust settings and permissions as needed to maximize the benefits for your team.
